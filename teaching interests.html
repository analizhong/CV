Favorite Online Tools

  1. Kahoot

    A great online tool that enables teachers
    to create interactive quizzes played in real-time.
  2. Baamboozle

    This is like a game of Jeopardy, where students select numbered boxes and answer the question accordingly.

  3. Quizlet

    Students can use Quizlet both online and on their smartphone devices to review teacher made vocabulary sets or select premade sets created by others.

  4. Socrative

    An online quizzing option to take formative assessments of students' learning.
